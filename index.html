Sdenka Hair Design

Passion

Sdenka Ovalles is originally from Argentina. Has been in the beauty industry since 2000 and began as a hair stylist and make up artist in 2004. She specializes in trendy women & men cuts. New color trends and affirm relaxers. She is certified keratin treatment specialist and Fusion Hair Extension specialist. Sdenka is known for her Bridal & Formal Hair Design & Make up services throughout the DC, Maryland, Virginia area. Each year Sdenka strives to obtain new advanced education and training to provide her clients with cutting edge styles and techniques to further develop her craft and deliver superior results to her clients.
