To dispatch an interpreter simply fill in the below details, call 703-409-7078, or email your request at info@eyethconsultantsllc.com. An Eyeth representative will gather all the pertnit details required to begin your formal request. Please have the following information available to finalize your request:

  1. Dates and times
  2. Location
  3. POC
  4. Event description
  5. Service Users name and role
  6. Security information if available
  7. Prep materials for interpreters

For instance you might say:
Our annual conference will be held on next month at the Hilton. One session will have a panel in which one of the panelists is deaf. Our company is having a two-day refresher course on our computer system. We have one deaf employee who will be participating.
